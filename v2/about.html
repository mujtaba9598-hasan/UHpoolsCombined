About Us

Our Story

For over 16 years, UH Pools & Gardens has been transforming outdoor spaces across the UAE with world-class pool construction, landscaping, and building services.

Meet Our CEO

Bilal Muhammad

With over 16 years of hands-on experience in construction, landscaping, and pool building, Bilal Muhammad founded UH Pools & Gardens with a singular mission: to bring world-class outdoor living experiences to homes and businesses across the UAE.

Under his leadership, the company has grown from a small landscaping operation to a full-service firm handling everything from luxury swimming pools and garden design to building renovation and maintenance.

Bilal personally oversees every major project, ensuring that the quality, craftsmanship, and attention to detail that built the company's reputation are maintained on every job site.
