Dubai Hills Estate Pool

Design and construction of a luxury infinity pool with integrated landscape, outdoor lighting, and pool equipment room.

HomePortfolioDubai Hills Estate Pool
Client: Private ResidenceDubai Hills Estate, Dubai3 months
Infinity Pool Construction3D Design VisualizationLandscape IntegrationLED LightingPool Equipment Installation

A homeowner in Dubai Hills Estate approached us to build a show stopping infinity pool that would become the focal point of their villa's outdoor area. The brief demanded a seamless connection between the pool, garden, and entertainment zones.

The Challenge

The villa's backyard had a significant slope, which needed to be engineered into a feature rather than a problem. The client also wanted a vanishing edge that created the illusion of water flowing into the garden below.

Our Solution

Our engineers designed a multi level approach: the infinity pool sits on the upper terrace with a vanishing edge that overflows into a concealed catch basin. The lower level was landscaped with tropical plantings and a stone pathway. LED lighting was embedded in the pool walls and deck for dramatic nighttime effects.
