Pergola & Gazebo

Outdoor Living Structures

Custom-designed pergolas, gazebos, and shade structures that extend your living space and elevate your outdoor experience.

Structure Types

We design and install a range of outdoor structures, each engineered for Dubai's climate and your lifestyle.

Wooden Pergolas

Warm, natural timber pergolas that add rustic charm to any outdoor space. Built with treated hardwoods for long-lasting beauty in Dubai's climate.

  • Premium treated hardwood construction
  • Custom dimensions and stain finishes
  • Climbing plant support frameworks
  • Integrated lighting options

Aluminum Pergolas

Sleek, modern aluminum pergolas with adjustable louvers for precise sun and rain control. Zero maintenance and built to withstand extreme heat.

  • Adjustable louvered roof systems
  • Powder-coated corrosion resistance
  • Motorized opening and closing
  • Rain sensor automation available

Garden Gazebos

Elegant standalone structures that create a focal point in your garden. Perfect for dining, relaxation, or entertaining guests outdoors.

  • Octagonal and rectangular designs
  • Built-in seating and planters
  • Weather-resistant roofing materials
  • Electrical wiring for lighting and fans

Shade Structures

Functional shade solutions for playgrounds, parking areas, pool decks, and large gathering spaces. Engineered for maximum coverage and durability.

  • HDPE and PVC membrane options
  • Steel and aluminum frameworks
  • UV protection up to 98%
  • Custom shapes and color matching

Why Invest in Outdoor Structures

Beyond aesthetics, pergolas and gazebos deliver real functional and financial benefits for your property.

UV Protection

Our structures block up to 98% of harmful UV rays, letting you enjoy the outdoors safely all year round without worrying about sun exposure.

Property Value

A well-designed pergola or gazebo can increase your property's market value by enhancing curb appeal and adding functional outdoor living space.

Built to Last

Every structure we build uses corrosion-resistant materials and engineering-grade fasteners to withstand Dubai's harsh summers and occasional storms.

From concept to construction, we create outdoor structures that complement your property and lifestyle. Talk to our team today.
