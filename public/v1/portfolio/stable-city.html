Case Study

Stable City Villa Compound

Complete outdoor transformation for a multi villa compound including landscaping, swimming pools, pergolas, and irrigation systems.

Home/Portfolio/Stable City Villa Compound
Client: Stable City Development
Dubai
6 months
Villa LandscapingSwimming Pool ConstructionPergola InstallationIrrigation SystemsOutdoor Lighting

Stable City is a premium villa compound in Dubai that required a cohesive outdoor environment across multiple properties. UH Pools & Gardens was contracted to deliver a unified landscape design with individual pool installations for each villa.

The Challenge

The primary challenge was creating a consistent aesthetic across the compound while accommodating each villa owner's individual preferences. The project also required completing all work within a tight 6 month timeline to coincide with the handover schedule.

Our Solution

We developed a master landscape plan with shared design language (materials, plant palette, lighting style) while allowing customization at each villa. Pools were designed with a common infinity edge style but varied in size and depth. Smart irrigation was installed compound wide with individual zone controls.
