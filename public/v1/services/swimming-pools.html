Pool Types

8 Types of Swimming Pools

Every property is unique. We build the pool that fits your space, style, and lifestyle.

Infinity Pool

Vanishing edge design that blends seamlessly with the horizon, perfect for villas with a view.

Lap Pool

Long, narrow pools designed for swimming laps and fitness, ideal for health conscious homeowners.

Plunge Pool

Compact, deep pools perfect for cooling off in smaller spaces without sacrificing style.

Overflow Pool

Water flows over one or more edges into a catch basin, creating a dramatic visual effect.

Indoor Pool

Temperature controlled pools built within enclosed structures for year round enjoyment.

Natural Pool

Chemical free pools using biological filtration and plants for a natural swimming experience.

Rooftop Pool

Elevated pools designed for penthouses and high rise villas with structural engineering expertise.

Kids Pool

Shallow, safe pools with fun features designed specifically for children and families.
